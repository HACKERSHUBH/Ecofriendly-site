RECYCLE EXTRA PRODUCT

In this Game, Webpage Is Divided For two person. One Is The Perfect Citizen as He Continously Uses Recycling and reduces the extra product that is not needed While another one i.e. you, is Not a Good Citizen.In Order To Be a Good Citizen,You Have To Recycle The Extra Paper Which you have instead Of trashing it. This Can Be Done By Clicking On The Extra Paper Appears On your Column(left side).
